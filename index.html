Missing local records of regionally common species

A tool to help recorders identify local gaps in iNaturalist species records. The tool builds a list of species that have been recorded within 5km of where you are. The tool determines which of these species haven't been recorded within 1km of where you are to help you prioritise which species are most important to record to fill data gaps.
